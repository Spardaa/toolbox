ℹ️ About Hash Functions

MD5

128-bit hash, fast but not cryptographically secure. Used for checksums and non-security purposes.

SHA-1

160-bit hash, deprecated for security. Still used in Git and legacy systems.

SHA-256

256-bit hash, widely used for security. Part of SHA-2 family, recommended for most uses.

SHA-512

512-bit hash, more secure for large data. Part of SHA-2 family, best for high-security needs.
